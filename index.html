About Me

I'm Nathan and I'm creating content!

Hi everyone, I am Nathan Maney! I just finished my degree in History and Certificate in Digital Studies after a 10 year break from school. I am excited to share new skills I have learned with the world, and apply my skills from years of leadership in food and customer service to new media related industries. Additionally, I have ambitions to apply for graduate studies in Public History. This additional Masters degree would put me in the professional field of making publically consumable histories including videos, podcasts, and exhibits. Right now, I am most interested in monuments as part of historical representation, and how statues, public art and memorials contribute to our shared memories of the past.

I am driven by values of compassion and solidarity. I like people driven companies, non-profits and organizations which understand that people produce the most creative content, the most meaningful content, when their lives are full and nourished. I believe in the power of unions and other worker organizations which help people come together to have a seat at the table with employers.

As a means of developing experience in bulding assets, putting them into broadcasts, and editing the final products, I have been streaming on Twitch and YouTube as well as producing Minecraft related content as a hobby. Doing this is not only fun, but further develops my abilities in the field and pushes me to be savvy about social media presentation and analytics. I also plan to produce amateur public history content to build a portfolio before I enter graduate school. I am open to short term professional opportunities, and in addition I am open to inquiries about freelancing. Check out my resume section and some of my video content in the pages below!
